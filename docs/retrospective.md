# My Restrospective on this Project
Where I go over what went wrong, what went well, and what I'd do differently 
## What went well 
* The primary vision got delivered almost perfectly in my opinion
* The coding of the webpages went smooth for the most part
* I was able to share my thoughts and feelings smoothly
## What went wrong
* I couldn't get the pictures to the exact size I wanted them to be
* I made many typos in coding that frustrated me when I couldn't catch them
* Certain things were formatted really odd and I didn't understand why until hours later. 
## What I'd do differently 
* Create better navigation
* Format the pages a bit more stylishly 
* Create more pages, such as a page for each genre of game and go into more depth regarding examples and any sub-genres they have.
